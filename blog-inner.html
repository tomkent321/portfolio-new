Computer Based Training Pros and Cons

Posted 22 August 2018

I just had to sit through one of the scheduled training courses required by my company that is delivered through a computer based training system. I know this type of training is probably the cheapest and easiest to provide for a company with a lot of locations and a lot of employees.

But really! It has to be the most boring, mind-numbing, trite, demeaning exercize I have to go through all year! In this case the "medium is the message" as Marshal McLuhan said many years ago. I just have to belive that there must be a way to design computer based training so that it's really interesting.

Experts in education say that retention of poorly designed computer training is half that of well-designed training. The short term saving by using computer based training is often offset by the degredation of performance of those being trained.
